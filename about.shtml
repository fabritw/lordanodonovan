Company Profile

Lordan & O’Donovan Building contractors was established in 2009 when Patrick Lordan (qualified carpenter with 25 years experience) and Brian O’Donovan (qualified plasterer with 17 years experience) formed a partnership combining their expertise in delivering exceptional quality building workmanship.

We are passionate about what we do and how we do it. We have a reputation for consistently delivering exceptional quality that surpasses the expectations of all our clients.

This is reflected in the level of repeat business we have secured.

We strive to foster good professional working relationships with our clients and anticipate and respond to their requirements.

Most of our work has come about as a result of us being recommended to others by present and past clients.

We differentiate ourselves from our competitors in many ways including :

  • We have a wealth of experience in all aspects of the construction process from demolition, standard block building, pre fabricated timber frame and steel frame buildings and delivering a superior quality turn key finish in both new build and renovation projects.

  • We work with our clients to ensure their satisfaction at every stage of the construction process.

  • We use high quality materials sourced from our core group of builders providers.

  • We have a core group of loyal and highly skilled tradesmen and subcontractors.

  • We ensure that all workers comply with health and safety regulations.

  • The directors of the company are hands on workers with the passion, skill, motivation and reputation to drive their building team and sub contractors forward to achieve excellence in quality. Thus delivering an exceptional quality finish that exceeds our clients expectations.

  • As working directors on site, our clients are assured of an extra diligent approach to their project.

  • We are always available to our clients and can be contacted at any time, day or night.
